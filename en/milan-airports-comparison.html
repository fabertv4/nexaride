FAQ

Which is the main airport of Milan?+
Milan-Malpensa (MXP) is Milan's main and largest airport, the hub for long-haul and intercontinental flights. Linate (LIN) is the city airport for domestic and short-haul European routes. Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY), although 45 km from Milan, serves the metropolitan area as a low-cost hub.
How far is Malpensa from Milan center?+
About 50 km. Typical driving time: 50-70 minutes depending on traffic and time of day.
How far is Linate from Milan center?+
About 7 km. It is the city airport: by car it takes 15-25 minutes.
How far is Orio al Serio from Milan?+
About 45-50 km. Typical driving time: 50-60 minutes via A4 motorway.
Which airport should I choose for a business trip?+
Linate for same-day trips thanks to its proximity to the center. Malpensa if the flight is international. Orio al Serio is competitive for low-cost flights.
Is private transfer better than public transport?+
Private NCC transfer is convenient for groups (2+ passengers), late-night flights with limited train/bus availability, bulky luggage, or when you want to arrive directly at the address without changes.
