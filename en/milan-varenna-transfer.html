Varenna, the jewel of the east shore

Varenna is one of the most picturesque villages on Lake Como, on the east shore. Reachable from Milan in 1h15 by car via SS36, it's the perfect base for the famous "central lake triangle" (Bellagio, Menaggio, Varenna — connected by ferry in 10–15 min). A private chauffeur transfer beats the train for groups, luggage, and late-night flights.

Trenord train vs private transfer — honest comparison

Unlike Bellagio or Cernobbio, Varenna has a train station (Varenna-Esino). So the train is a real option. Here's the honest comparison:

Frequently asked questions

How far is Varenna from Milan?+
~70 km from Milan center, 1h15 typical via SS36 (Statale Lecco). From Malpensa ~95 km (1h40), from Linate ~75 km (1h20), from Bergamo Orio al Serio ~90 km (1h30).
Trenord train Milan–Varenna or private transfer?+
Varenna-Esino is served by Trenord trains from Milano Centrale (1h–1h30). For solo travellers with light luggage the train is competitive. For groups of 2+ passengers, families, bulky luggage or late-night flights, a private chauffeur transfer is more comfortable and direct.
Which hotels do you serve in Varenna?+
We operate for all Varenna hotels: Hotel Du Lac Varenna, Albergo Milano, Hotel Villa Cipressi, Hotel Beretta, Hotel Royal Victoria, and private villas. Meet & Greet service at reception.
Direct airport transfer to Varenna?+
Yes. Malpensa–Varenna ~1h40, Linate–Varenna ~1h20, Bergamo Orio al Serio–Varenna ~1h30. Door-to-door service, flight monitoring, fixed price.
Is Varenna the best base to visit Lake Como?+
Varenna is one of the most picturesque villages on Lake Como, on the east shore. From Varenna you can easily reach Bellagio (10–15 min by ferry) and Menaggio (15 min), forming the famous "central lake triangle". An excellent base to visit the entire Lake Como.
Do drivers speak English?+
Yes, our chauffeurs speak English. Other languages on request when booking.
