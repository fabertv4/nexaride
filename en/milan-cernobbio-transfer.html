Frequently asked questions

How far is Cernobbio from Milan?+
~55 km from Milan center, 1h typical via A9. From Malpensa ~1h, from Linate ~1h05, from Bergamo Orio al Serio ~1h25.
Do you serve Villa d'Este?+
Yes, regularly. Plus Mandarin Oriental, Hilton Lake Como and all luxury hotels. Meet & Greet at reception included on request.
Can I reach Cernobbio by train?+
No direct station. Como San Giovanni (5 km, 15 minutes by car) is the closest. A private transfer is faster and more direct, with no changes.
Direct transfer from the airport?+
Yes. Malpensa-Cernobbio ~1h, Linate-Cernobbio ~1h05, Orio-Cernobbio ~1h25. Door-to-door service, chauffeur waiting at the gate.
Service for Concorso d'Eleganza or Ambrosetti Forum?+
Yes — we offer dedicated Business-class service. Advance booking is strongly recommended for event days.
English-speaking drivers?+
Yes, our chauffeurs speak English. Other languages on request when booking.
