From Malpensa Airport to Bellagio

Milan Malpensa (MXP) is the airport most travellers use to reach Bellagio and Lake Como — and the drive is shorter than many expect.

By car, Malpensa to Bellagio is about 82 km: around 1h15 without traffic, realistically 1h30–1h35 door-to-door. Public transport is the slow way round: there is no direct train, so you would take a train with a change at Saronno towards Como and then the C30 bus to Bellagio (about 2h48 in total), or the hydrofoil from Como (45–50 minutes, roughly every 2 hours — about 3h13 in total). With luggage, allow about 3 hours.

FAQ

How far is Bellagio from Milan?+
~72–75 km from Milan center, about 1h15 by route planners and closer to 1h30 in practice, via A9 or SS583. From Malpensa ~1h30–1h35, from Linate ~1h35.
How far is Bellagio from Malpensa Airport?+
About 82 km by road. A private transfer takes around 1h15 without traffic — realistically 1h30–1h35 door-to-door. Public transport needs at least two changes and around 3 hours.
Is there a direct train from Malpensa to Bellagio?+
No — Bellagio has no railway station and there is no direct line from Malpensa. By public transport you take a train with a change at Saronno towards Como, then the C30 bus to Bellagio (about 2h48 in total), or the hydrofoil from Como (45–50 minutes, roughly every 2 hours — about 3h13 in total). Realistically allow about 3 hours.
Can I reach Bellagio by train?+
No — there's no station. You arrive by car or by ferry from Como/Varenna. By train: Como or Varenna + ferry/transfer.
