FAQ

How long does it take to get from Malpensa to Milan center?

50-70 minutes by car, ~50 minutes by Malpensa Express train, 60-75 minutes by bus.

What is the fastest way?

Malpensa Express is the most predictable (no traffic). NCC and taxi are comparable in time but door-to-door.

How much does a taxi from Malpensa cost?

The fixed fare for official taxis is €110 (Milan city law). Possible supplements for luggage, night, holidays.

How much is a private NCC from Malpensa?

Depends on vehicle, time, destination, passengers. Message us on WhatsApp with route+date+time: fixed price quote in 2-5 minutes.

Train or private transfer?

Train for single travellers with light luggage, daytime, central destination. NCC for groups 2+, luggage, night-time, non-central hotel.

Are there buses from the airport?

Yes: Malpensa Shuttle, Terravision, AirportBus. Cost €10-12, time 60-75 min, departures every 20-30 min to Milan Centrale.
