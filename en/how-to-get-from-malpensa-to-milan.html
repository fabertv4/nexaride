FAQ

How long does it take to get from Malpensa to Milan center?+
By car (taxi or NCC) typical time is 50–70 minutes depending on traffic. The Malpensa Express train reaches Milan Cadorna or Centrale in about 50 minutes. Shuttle buses take 60–75 minutes.
What is the fastest way from Malpensa to Milan?+
The Malpensa Express train (~50 min to Cadorna or Centrale, with departures every 30 minutes) is usually the fastest and most predictable since it's not affected by traffic. Car and bus require 50–75 minutes in ideal conditions, but can be longer during rush hour. NCC and taxi are door-to-door.
How much does a taxi from Malpensa to Milan cost?+
The fixed fare set for official licensed taxis from Milan-Malpensa to the city center is around €110, mandated by law. Supplements for luggage, holidays and night-time may apply. To avoid surprises, a private NCC transfer with a fixed price confirmed before the trip is recommended.
How much does a private NCC chauffeur cost from Malpensa?+
The price depends on vehicle (Comfort, Business, Van), time, exact destination and number of passengers. For a fast fixed-price quote, message us on WhatsApp with route, date and time — typical reply in 2–5 minutes.
Should I take the train or a private transfer from Malpensa?+
The train is convenient for single travellers with light luggage arriving at central stations (Cadorna or Centrale) during daytime hours. The NCC transfer is better for groups (2+ people), bulky luggage, late-night flights when the train stops running, hotels not near the stations, or when you want to avoid changing vehicles.
Are there buses from Malpensa airport?+
Yes, several operators (Malpensa Shuttle, Terravision, AirportBus, Caronte) connect Malpensa with Milan Centrale and other destinations. Typical cost is €10–12, time 60–75 minutes, departures every 20–30 minutes.
