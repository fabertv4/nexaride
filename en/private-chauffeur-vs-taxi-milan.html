Private Chauffeur vs Taxi in Milan: practical comparison

Fares, booking, comfort and regulations. Which one to pick for airports, the city and longer routes — explained for international travellers.

In Italy you can choose between two regulated professional ground transport options: taxi (metered, street-hail or app) and NCC — Noleggio Con Conducente, a private hire / chauffeur service that operates by advance booking only. This guide explains the practical differences so you can choose the right option for your situation.

When the taxi is the right choice

  • ✓ Short urban trip, no booking needed.
  • ✓ Solo traveller with light luggage.
  • ✓ Daytime, central Milan with plenty of taxis at every stand.
  • ✓ Malpensa–Milan centre route at €110 fixed (city ordinance) — comparable to NCC for a single passenger.

When a private chauffeur (NCC) is better

  • ✓ Fixed price in advance — no surprises, no language barrier on the meter.
  • ✓ Night flights, early morning, holidays when taxis are scarce.
  • ✓ Groups of 5+ passengers — Van service available (taxis cap at 4).
  • ✓ Meet & Greet at the airport — driver waiting with a name sign, helps with luggage.
  • ✓ Longer routes: Lake Como, Bergamo Orio al Serio, business events outside Milan.
  • ✓ Business / VIP comfort — Mercedes-Benz fleet, professional dress code, discretion.
  • ✓ English-speaking driver guaranteed — important for international travellers.
  • ✓ Invoicing for business expense reporting.

Common misconceptions

✕ "I can hail an NCC on the street like Uber"

By Italian law NCC operates only on advance booking, departing from a licensed garage. They cannot pick up passengers on the street. This isn't a service limitation — it's a legal regulation.

✕ "Uber works the same way as in the US"

In Italy Uber operates only as Uber Black, a partnership with NCC licence holders. It's not the same as the US service — booking required, fixed-price logic, no UberX in most cities.

✕ "Private chauffeur is always more expensive"

For 2+ passengers or longer routes the price is comparable to a taxi, with the advantage of knowing the cost in advance and getting better vehicles.

FAQ

What is NCC exactly?+
"Noleggio Con Conducente" — private hire vehicle / professional chauffeur service. Licensed by municipalities, regulated under law 21/1992. Pre-booked only, fixed price.
When is taxi better?+
Short urban trips, no booking, solo travel, daytime. Fixed Malpensa–Milan centre fare of €110 is also competitive.
When is private chauffeur better?+
Night flights, groups, luggage, longer routes, business needs, English-speaking driver guaranteed.
Cost comparison?+
Short trips: taxi cheaper. Longer/airport/group routes: comparable, but private chauffeur is fixed-price upfront.
Can I street-hail NCC?+
No — by law it's advance booking only. WhatsApp is the fastest channel.
Payment methods?+
Cards, cash, bank transfer, Apple/Google Pay accepted by NexaRide.
