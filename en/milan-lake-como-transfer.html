Private transfer vs train: which to choose?

Lake Como is accessible by Trenord from Milano Cadorna (to Como Lago) or Milano Centrale (to Como San Giovanni and Varenna). The train works well if you're a solo traveller heading to Como town or Varenna — but it has limitations:

  • • Bellagio, Tremezzo, Menaggio are NOT directly reached by train — you'd need bus + ferry combinations
  • • Travelling with luggage on Italian trains is uncomfortable, especially in summer
  • • For groups of 3+ people the per-person cost of a private transfer is competitive with train tickets
  • • Train schedules don't always match flight arrivals, especially late at night
