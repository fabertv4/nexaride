Private transfer vs train: which to choose?

Lake Como is accessible by Trenord from Milano Cadorna (to Como Lago) or Milano Centrale (to Como San Giovanni and Varenna). The train works well if you're a solo traveller heading to Como town or Varenna — but it has limitations:

  • •Bellagio, Tremezzo, Menaggio are NOT directly reached by train — you'd need bus + ferry combinations.
  • •Travelling with luggage on Italian trains is uncomfortable, especially in summer.
  • •For groups of 3+ people the per-person cost of a private transfer is competitive with train tickets.
  • •Train schedules don't always match flight arrivals, especially late at night.

FAQ

How far is Milan from Lake Como?+
Como town is about 50 km from Milan (50-60 minutes by car via the A9 motorway). Bellagio is about 80 km (1h30). Cernobbio is about 55 km (1h). Varenna is about 85 km (1h30). Distances vary for other lakeside towns.
Can I book a transfer from the airport directly to Lake Como?+
Yes. From Malpensa (MXP) to Como: about 50 km (1h). From Linate (LIN): about 55 km (1h10). From Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY): about 75 km (1h20). We organize the direct transfer with fixed-price quote.
Which Lake Como destinations do you serve?+
Como, Cernobbio, Moltrasio, Laglio, Carate Urio, Argegno, Sala Comacina, Tremezzo, Lenno, Mezzegra, Bellagio, Varenna, Menaggio, Gravedona, Domaso and the entire lake. For specific destinations, message us on WhatsApp.
Is a private transfer better than the train?+
For Como town the train (Trenord/SBB from Milano Cadorna or Centrale) is competitive for solo travellers. For Bellagio, Varenna, Tremezzo and other towns not directly reached by train, a private chauffeur transfer is the most comfortable and direct choice. Also better for groups and heavy luggage.
Can I book a chauffeur for a day tour of the lake?+
Yes, we offer hourly hire service (e.g. half-day or full-day) to visit multiple lake destinations with a driver who waits for you. Custom quote on WhatsApp.
Do drivers speak English?+
Yes, our chauffeurs speak English. For other languages (German, French, Spanish) please specify when booking — we'll match the right driver when possible.
