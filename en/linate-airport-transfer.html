Linate → Milan transfer: the closest airport to the city

Linate (LIN) is the closest airport to central Milan: about 7 km, 15–20 minutes by car. It is the ideal hub for domestic and European flights and for anyone who wants to reach the city in just a few minutes. With NexaRide the Linate–Milan city centre transfer starts from €60, a fixed price agreed in advance: your driver waits for you at arrivals with a sign showing your name, with flight tracking and waiting included.

  • ~7 km from the centre, 15–20 minutes
  • From €60 to Milan city centre — fixed price
  • Meet & greet at arrivals with a name sign
  • Linate Prime too for charter flights and private jets

Linate Prime too — private flights terminal

We also operate at Linate Prime, the terminal dedicated to charter flights, general aviation and private jets. Pickup and drop-off directly at the Prime gate, with flexible waiting and discreet luggage handling. Ideal for crews, charter flight passengers, executives and VIPs. Send us your flight and timing on WhatsApp: we track arrival/departure and coordinate with the handler.

  • Dedicated service at the Prime terminal (Viale Forlanini side)
  • Mercedes E-Class / V-Class for crews and groups
  • Discretion, punctuality, invoice for business

FAQ

How much is a transfer to/from Milan airports?+
Prices depend on the route and the number of passengers. Linate starts from €60. For Malpensa and Orio ask a quick quote on WhatsApp: we reply within minutes.
How can I pay?+
Cash, card or bank transfer. The price is agreed in advance and paid at the end of the ride: no upfront payment required to book.
Are there cancellation fees?+
No fees at all. Cancellation is always free: if your plans change, just let us know on WhatsApp.
Is airport waiting included?+
Yes. Your driver waits with a sign showing your name and waiting time is included in the fare, even if the flight is delayed: we track the arrival in real time.
Do you only do airports?+
No. We do transfers to and from airports, the city, Lake Como, hotels, events and long routes on request.
How do I book?+
Message us on WhatsApp with your trip details, or fill in the form above: we open WhatsApp with your details ready.
Do you track delayed flights?+
Yes, we track flights in real time and adjust pick-up time at no extra cost.
How much is the transfer from Linate to Milan?+
The Linate–Milan city centre transfer starts from €60, a fixed price agreed before you set off. For other routes we give you a quick quote on WhatsApp.
How far is Linate from Milan city centre?+
About 7 km: 15–20 minutes by car. It is the closest airport to the city.
Do you also cover Linate Prime for charter flights and private jets?+
Yes. We regularly operate at Linate Prime, the terminal dedicated to general aviation, charter flights and private jets. Pickup directly at the Prime gate, luggage handling and coordination with the handler. Send us your flight details on WhatsApp.
